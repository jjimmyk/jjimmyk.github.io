How To Totally Shred One Chord on the Guitar

  1. Clip an electronic Snark tuner on the head of the guitar.
  2. Using the tuning knobs, tune each string until the strings from largest to smallest play the notes E, A, D, G, B, E, respectively. Refer to the Snark tuner's display to view the note produced by each string.
  3. Congratulations! Your guitar is in tune.
