How To Sing Your First Opera Note

  1. Standup straight, with both feet firmly planted on the ground.
  2. Place your hands on your stomach.
  3. Inhale deeply for 5 seconds, filling your belly with air instead of your chest. You should feel your belly inflate.
  4. Exhale for 10 seconds, feeling your belly deflate.
  5. Inhale deeply again, feeling your belly fill up with air.
  6. Produce one, single note and hold it as long as you can, using the air held in your belly to sustain the note.
