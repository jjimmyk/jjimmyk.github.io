How To Standup Paddleboard

  1. While in shallow water next to your board, lay belly-down atop the board.
  2. Slowly inch your knees forward and move your arms backward until you are in a kneeling position.
  3. At this point, you should be kneeling atop the board. Detach the paddle from the board.
  4. Grasp the center of the paddle with your non-dominant hand, grasping the handle of the paddle with your dominant hand.
  5. Submerge the end piece of the paddle in the water on either side of the board.
  6. Pull the paddle backward to propel the board forward.
  7. Move the paddle to the opposite side of the board.
  8. Pull the paddle backward to propel the board forward.
  9. Repeat this process as necessary to move and steer the board as you wish.
  10. After you feel comfortable with moving and steering the board, quickly move one foot, quickly followed by the other foot, to a standing position. Keep your knees bent.
  11. Now you are standing on the board and may move and steer it throughout the water! Congrats!
